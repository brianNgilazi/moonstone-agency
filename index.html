Turning Passion Into Possibilities

Moonstone is a black female owned agency founded by Onalenna Shasha Ntsoko and Mampotla Machaba, whose two cofounders collectively have over 18 years experience in the entertainment, events and PR Industry. Prior to starting Moonstone Agency, they have worked with both locally and internationally renowned brands, artists and events. They pride themselves on finding innovative solutions in an agile manner for their clients, in an everchanging landscape.

The agency offers talent management, PR, event planning and management services, whether it be connecting artists to brands, brands to people or people to artists. Maximising how these connect is what lies at the heart of their 4P Principle - Passion, Purpose, People and Profit. This principle enables them to create endless possibilities for your brand.

In today’s multi-faceted world, we believe that it takes a combination of expertise and skills to truly stand out. That applies to both individuals and organisations, which is why Moonstone Agency offers talent management, PR, and event planning and management services.

At Moonstone Agency, we realise that everything is connected. Artists connected to brands. Brands connected to people. People connected to artists. This realisation is what has led us to develop our 4P Principle – Passion, Purpose, People and Profit. Embracing how these four pillars link to each other is what enables us to spot opportunities and turn passion into possibilities.

PASSION
We don’t just do what we do, we live it. Being as passionate about the industry and our business as we are means that we will be as passionate about yours and go all out when it comes to delivering for you.

PURPOSE
If there is no purpose, there is no point. We work to fully understand your purpose, in order to ensure it aligns with your consumers’ passion points in a relevant and authentic manner. Whether you need your purpose defined, made clear or propelled, Moonstone Agency will help you get there.

PEOPLE
Once we understand your brand purpose, we’ll help you resonate with your audience by looking at and understanding what they want, what they need, and how we can help you provide that to them.

PROFIT
By connecting Passion, Purpose and People, it will enable you to reach a new or wider audience, with the potential to access more revenue streams.
